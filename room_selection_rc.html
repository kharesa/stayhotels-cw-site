This establishment provides guests with limitless luxury while in London. GUESTROOMS & SUITES offer a prolonged respite from the city. Resting in bed or reading on a chaise - surrounded by the simple yet pristine. Ethereal at daybreak and halo gold at dusk, each accommodation is a celebration of contemporary design, styled with a nod to French tradition.

Our largest event space, easily accommodating up to 200 delegates theatre style, the Walpole Ballroom benefits from flexible break-out spaces and plenty of natural light.

Ideal for smaller meetings and business events, the characterful Lord Nelson Room is bathed in natural light with views over King’s Lynn’s Tuesday Market Place, and hosts up to 60 delegates theatre style.

79 stylish rooms offer special rates for business delegates, including full English and continental breakfast. For further details, contact our Events team.
